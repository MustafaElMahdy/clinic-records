The Challenge

Running a clinic is hard enough.

Paper folders, scattered spreadsheets, and legacy software slow you down — and put your patients at risk.

Paper records pile up

Files get lost, damaged, or simply become impossible to search through as your practice grows.

Disorganized visits

Diagnoses, prescriptions, and notes scattered across notebooks with no clear timeline or structure.

No audit trail

No way to know who accessed a record, made a change, or when — a liability risk for any practice.

Duplicate patients

The same patient registered twice under different spellings — creating conflicting records and confusion.

The Solution

DocuMed brings order to your clinic.

A single, elegant system purpose-built for private practices — handling everything from patient intake to visit documentation, file management, and team access, all with a clean interface your staff will actually want to use.

✓ Structured patient profiles
✓ Complete visit history

Features

Everything your practice needs.

Built feature by feature around the real workflow of a busy clinic.

Patient Management

Create structured patient profiles with full demographics, contact details, and medical identifiers.

  • Instant search by name, phone, or national ID
  • Clean profile view with complete history
  • Sex, date of birth, address — all in one place

Visit Tracking

Document every encounter with rich clinical notes, structured findings, and treatment plans.

  • Detailed clinical notes per visit
  • Diagnosis, prescription, and treatment fields
  • Chronological visit timeline

File Attachments

Upload and securely store lab results, X-rays, referral letters, and any document linked to a patient.

  • PDFs, images, and documents
  • Authenticated downloads only
  • Files scoped to clinic — never cross-visible

Role-Based Access

Every team member gets the right level of access — no more, no less.

  • Doctors, assistants, and admins
  • Granular permissions per action
  • Clinic admin manages their own users

Audit Logging

A tamper-evident log of every action taken in the system — who did what, and when.

  • View, edit, create, and delete events
  • Per-patient audit history
  • Compliance-ready from day one

Duplicate Detection

Before a new patient is saved, DocuMed automatically checks for likely duplicates.

  • Smart matching by phone & national ID
  • Fuzzy name similarity check
  • Warning shown before saving — never blocked

Multi-Clinic Ready

Designed from day one for practices with multiple branches or a growing team.

  • Full clinic-level data isolation
  • Users belong to exactly one clinic
  • Scales as your practice grows

Privacy by Design

Patient data is never exposed by accident — every access point is authenticated and clinic-scoped.

  • No direct file URL access
  • Session-based authentication
  • Role checks on every action
